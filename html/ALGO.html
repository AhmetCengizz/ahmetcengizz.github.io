What is Algorand (ALGO)?

Algorand; It is a decentralized blockchain-based network that supports multiple applications and can work on its own. These systems are safe, scalable and efficient to deliver effective results in real-world use cases. Algorand will support calculations that need unequivocal performance guarantees to establish the next generation trust standard. The Algorand mainnet was launched in June 2019, and as of December 2020, it could process approximately 1 million transactions per day.

Why is Algorand Different?

Algorand, which emerged as a solution to the problem of slow transactions in Bitcoin and other blockchain networks, was developed to increase speed and efficiency. Since Algorand is built on the permissionless Proof-of-Stake blockchain protocol, it does not require the energy-intensive mining process required for Bitcoin production and is designed to operate with much lower transaction fees.
