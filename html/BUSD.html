What is BUSD?

The Binance USD (BUSD) currency is a one-to-one US dollar pegged stablecoin developed in partnership with Binance exchange and Paxos, approved and regulated by the New York State Department of Financial Services (NYDFS). Monthly Audit Report published for BUSD can be followed on the official website. Launched on September 5, 2019, BUSD is a project that aims to combine the value stability offered by the US dollar with blockchain technology. The digital fiat currency developed based on ERC-20 also supports the BEP-2 standard.

BUSD Usage Scenarios

Stablecoin, thanks to its price stability; It can be used in transfer transactions, payments and reconciliation agreements, as well as playing an important role in Decentralized Finance (DeFi). BUSD use cases include:

  • • Thanks to the blockchain, digital dollars (BUSD) can be transferred to any location at a very low cost and within minutes.
  • • BUSD can be traded on different exchanges and DEX protocols.
  • • Interest income can be obtained by investing BUSD.
  • • Goods and services can be paid using BUSD.
  • • BUSD currency can be used as collateral and loan product.
  • • BUSD can be used as cross collateral in futures transactions.
  • • BUSD currency can be stored on an exchange or digital wallet.
