What is Uniswap (UNI)?

Uniswap is a popular decentralized trading protocol known for its role in facilitating automated trading of decentralized finance (DeFi) tokens. Uniswap, an example of an automated market maker (AMM), was launched in November 2018, but has garnered considerable attention this year thanks to the popularity of DeFi protocols and the rise in token trading associated with it. Uniswap aims to increase the efficiency of token trading compared to its examples on traditional exchanges, as well as to offer automated transactions and a fully open platform to everyone who owns the token. Uniswap creates greater efficiency by solving liquidity problems in automated solutions and escapes the problems encountered in early decentralized exchanges. Uniswap went a step further by awarding these tokens to former users of the protocol, as it developed its own governance token, UNI, in September 2020. This move not only provided profit potential to users, but also allowed users to shape the future of the protocol, which makes decentralized assets attractive.

Why is Uniswap Different?

Uniswap exists to build liquidity into the DeFi space, hence the value provided by trading and trading. The protocol, which is one of the actively used AMM solutions, works with the formula (X * Y = K) developed for the autonomous stock market. Founder Hayden Adams describes himself as the inventor of the Uniswap exclusive application of the formula. Uniswap is not just a decentralized exchange; Uniswap is a protocol that also tries to solve the liquidity problems experienced by platforms such as EtherDelta. It promotes efficiency by automating the market making process, limiting risk and reducing costs for all parties. The mechanism also removes identity requirements for users, and technically anyone can create liquidity pools for any token pair. According to Uniswap, their own governance token (UNI) was launched with the motto "To elevate the network with community-owned and self-sustainable infrastructure, while continuing to flawlessly preserve the indestructible and autonomous qualities of Uniswap."
